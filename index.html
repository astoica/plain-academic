I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Columbia University in 2022, advised by Augustin Chaintreau. I spent Fall 2022 as a Simons Fellow at the Simons Instiute for Theory of Computing in Berkeley, attending the Graph Limits and Processes on Networks program.
